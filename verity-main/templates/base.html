About RealTalk

RealTalk analyzes news headlines for emotional bias using natural language processing techniques.

How it works:
  • Headlines are fetched from a public News API
  • Each headline is analyzed using NLP to detect emotional bias
  • A bias score and category is assigned based on:
    • Sentiment analysis (positive/negative tone)
    • Subjectivity detection
    • Presence of emotionally charged words
  • Special attention is given to war-related news (Russia-Ukraine, etc.) with additional bias detection
Bias Categories:
Neutral Low emotional content, factual presentation
Slightly Biased Some emotional language or framing
Highly Biased Strong emotional language or framing
